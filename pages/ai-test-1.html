The HR Screening Tool has no mechanism for an authorised human to immediately halt the system's operation. Art. 14(4)(e) requires that oversight persons can interrupt the system using a "stop" button or similar procedure.

F-002: No automation bias safeguards

Critical

There are no measures to prevent HR staff from over-relying on the AI's candidate rankings without independent review. Art. 14(4)(b) requires measures to prevent automation bias.

Remediation: Add mandatory human review checkpoints before shortlisting. Display confidence scores alongside rankings. Require reviewers to confirm they have independently assessed each candidate. Estimated effort: 3-5 days.

Critical
